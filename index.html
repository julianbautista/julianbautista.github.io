Welcome to my personal page

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Centre de Physique des Particules de Marseille, France.


My main interest is observational cosmology. Here a list of topics that I am interested in:

  • Dark energy
  • Large-scale structures
  • Baryon acoustic oscillations
  • Redshift-space distortions
  • Galaxies
  • Lyman-alpha forest
  • Spectroscopic surveys
  • Peculiar velocities
  • Type-Ia Supernovae

Here are the projects that I am involved with:

  • Sloan Digital Sky Survey (SDSS)
  • Dark Energy Spectroscopic Instrument (DESI)
  • Vera Rubin Observatory Legacy Survey of Space and Time (LSST)
  • Zwicky Transient Facility (ZTF)
